Laser Tattoo Removal

 Getting rid of tattoos was quite cumbersome earlier as it involved various painful techniques. Now the advent of new technology has made this procedure more effective and less painful. Out of all the methods available today, laser technology is most beneficial and efficacious. Energy emitted by a particular wavelength of laser passes through the skin and breaks tattoo ink into multiple fragments which are slowly removed by the body's lymphatic system.

Q SWITCH
There are various lasers in the market, but Q-switch lasers (Nd:YAG, Alexandrite and Ruby laser) are the most efficacious and safe lasers to remove tattoos of any colour. Each of these lasers give excellent results targeting different colours at different wavelengths.

While Alexandrite and Ruby laser works best for green and blue colour respectively, black colour is targeted by all three lasers effectively. Number of sessions required varies on many factors such as size and tattoo pigment, but on an average 3-4 sessions of Q-switch lasers 6-8 weeks apart give very good results.

Acne
Poking your pimples, ignoring it and closing your eyes at the problem will do no good rather it will leave your face with more scars and pigmentation.
What Causes Acne
Acne/pimple is a very common condition affecting mostly teenage and young adults and doesn’t always require a specialist to diagnose it. You know, you have it when your skin develops blackheads, whiteheads, or red or yellow spots and becomes greasy. Acne doesn’t care who you are, what colour your skin is or how old you are. However, it is far more likely that, as you approach puberty, your skin will start to change enough to kick-start the ‘acne process’. What most people don’t know is that you don’t always grow out of it: 15 percent of women in their 40s are still troubled by acne, although for men it is less likely to carry on for so long.


It’s a cruel fact that acne feels most at home on your face, but it can also crop up on your neck, back, chest or shoulders. This is because the oil-producing glands (the sebaceous glands) on your skin are most concentrated here. Sometimes pimples are so painful and so severe that it takes down sleep and confidence. Very often they are so notorious that no matter what treatment or home remedies you take, it always comes back. Even there may be a strong urge to poke and squeeze pimples leaving behind marks and scars on your face. So for all those who are tired and want to give up on pimples, here comes the good news . Pimple is absolutely treatable. We at SKINVIBES, believe in treating the root cause of pimples with our customized treatment protocols along with chemical peels, lasers and light therapy and bring you a step closer to your vibrant, spotless skin.
Treatment for Acne
Medicines : Tablets. Selection of particular medicines depend on type and severity of acne. There are several medicines available to treat acne in the form of creams,gel,lotion,and medicinal treatment on an average 5-6 months are required for complete clearance of acne lesions.

Procedural treatment : Besides prescription medicines there are several procedures that can be done to treat acne. The benefits of these procedures are not only early resolutions of acne but it also clears pigmentation,reduces scars, redness, and gives a youthful bright skin. Overall, it also brings down the total cost of treatment of acne and its sequelae.
What We Can Do
CHEMICAL PEEL - A carefully selected professional peel such as Glycolic acid peel, Salicylic acid peel or combination peel are applied over skin once or twice in a month. In a few treatment sessions, you get back your youthful skin devoid of pimples and spots.
IPL - Also known as photo-facial is a great boon in the treatment of papulo-pustular acne. In this face is given few shots of pulsed light. It dramatically clears acne, reduces oil secretion, reduces pore size, decreases post acne redness
COMEDONE EXTRACTION - White-head or blackhead are also called comedones. Closed comedones are expressed to un-clog the pore. It facilitates delivery of creams and gels and reduces treatment duration.

Anti Ageing
Ageing (getting old) is an inevitable and continuous process and skin is the first organ to show its sign. But sometimes it’s not only ageing but several other external factors such as UV rays, pollution, cosmeceuticals, acne and other skin problems make the skin tone and texture uneven with spots, scars, wrinkles and dullness. Though we cannot stop ageing process, definitely we can reverse the signs of ageing skin giving a more young and beautiful skin through facial peels.
Facial peels also known as chemical peels is a procedure in which a non-toxic chemical solution mostly fruit extracts are applied over the face and body to even out the skin’s texture by removing the damaged outer layers. A facial chemical peel treatment eliminates the top layer of dead skin cells and energizes the growth of new cells and collagen through deep exfoliation. It provides fresher, cleaner, clearer, and brighter skin texture. Chemical peel solutions are of various types and correct selection of peel solution is most important to get the desired result. It also depends on skin types, sensitivity of skin and skin problems
WHAT ALL CAN BE TREATED WITH PEELS?

Facial peels are generally carried out on the face, neck, hands, and are effective in treating the following:

  • Wrinkles and fine lines from around the mouth and the eyes
  • Softening the deeper fine lines
  • Rectifying damages done to the tone and texture of the skin
  • Diminishing the signs of aging
  • Harmed skin cells
  • Age spots
  • Treating the damage caused due to exposure to the harmful rays of the sun
  • Dark patches, freckles, etc., caused due to pregnancy
  • Scaling down different types of acne

CARBON PEEL
Carbon Peel Facial also referred to as the ‘China Doll Peel Facial’ (named for its porcelain doll-like finish to the skin) is all time favourite lunchtime procedure of many Hollywood celebrities, hence popularly it is also known as Hollywood facial. This is an innovative, painless laser resurfacing treatment that utilizes focused light beams to treat skin imperfections without any downtime. First, we apply a thin layer of carbon to the skin’s surface. Once the charcoal preparation is evenly distributed, a focused light beam is administered for a precise targeted area. The carbon particles actively absorb the light and get removed along with damaged cells found in the upper layers of the skin. And this gives an instant skin glow with perfect texture and complexion.
The best part of the procedure is that as the treated skin regenerates, the appearance of uneven skin, dark or age spots and pigment are visibly diminished with better skin tone as well as lifting and tightening of skin.
It also removes pigmentation of the skin which develops for a number of different reasons including, sun damage, inflammation, hormonal changes, acne and reaction to some medications.

Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP)
Platelet Rich Plasma or more commonly known as PRP is a revolutionized process to initiate the healing process of the body. Plasma is one of three components of blood which contains a lot of platelets. When injected into skin, platelets release many growth factors into the skin which promote cell growth, collagen remodelling and rejuvenate the skin

PRP treatment includes so many steps.
  1. First patient is calmed,relaxed and the procedure explained.
  2. Cleaning and numbing of the affected area is done.
  3. Then in order to make PRP, a fixed amount of blood is collected from the patient's arm in specialized tubes. Amount of blood drawn depends upon the area to be treated but it hardly exceeds the amount required for a normal blood test.
  4. A centrifuge machine then processes the blood.
  5. After centrifugation, plasma is separated.
  6. Doctors inject the solution in a small amount to the affected area using a syringe and a needle.
  7. The injected area is then cleansed.
  8. After care
  9. These are very simple steps to be followed to get best results.
  10. There may be slight redness,swelling and pain at injection sites. Redness and swelling will subside in approx 30 minutes. Pain would be mild and will subside on its own. But if pain is unbearable then use of cold compression will decrease the post-injection pain. Use it for 10 to 20 minutes at a time.
  11. Do not take any anti-inflammatory pain medication on your own. Ask the Doctor.
  12. Protect the affected area with the best care


Does PRP really help? In what conditions PRPcan be beneficial?

Benefits & applications
  • Since the PRP process initiates the natural growth of cells, it works in many ways and helps to repair damaged areas of the body. It can be used for healing the skin, acne, stretch marks, and more. Since it is prepared from one’s own blood,chances of side-effects are minimal. Let’s take a look at its benefits.
For Skin Rejuvenation
  • Though PRP is mainly used by athletes to repair their injured tendons and tissues faster, it has lately been used by women to rejuvenate their skin. Since it stimulates collagen remodelling and improves the natural healing process, it offers an array of benefits for the skin also. Many find PRP helpful for toning their skin, improving the skin texture, removing the wrinkles and fine lines, minimizing the pores, and more.

Acne Scars
  • PRP has proved to be a boon in the management of acne scars. Acne scars happen mainly because of too much or too less production of the collagen of the skin. Since depressed acne scars occur because of less production of collagen, this treatment has proven to be beneficial. PRP when combined with LASER, microneedling, MNRF the results are much better.It improves the natural healing power, thus improving the scar and making it less noticeable.
Stretch Marks
  • For treating the stretch marks, PRP can be helpful in certain cases. PRP enhances the collagen production that replaces the dead cells with the new one. Further it decreases the size of stretch marks and makes it less noticeable.
Scars
  • For treating scars, PRP gets a huge success. It can heal the deep-pigmented scar, hyper-pigmented scar or shallow scar. It lessens the scar depth and helps you to get rid of it. With the collagen production power, it treats the problem effectively. Though PRP can be effective as a standalone procedure but sometimes it is combined with another procedure such as micro-needling or laser to get best results
Under Eye Dark Circles
  • Many dermatologists suggest PRP for under eye dark circles. It stimulates the production of collagen and elastin in the area and helps produce new cells, thus offering you a healthy, natural skin.
Wrinkles
  • PRP helps in self-reparation of the body by allowing the skin cells to regenerate the new layer of the skin tissue. It also aids your body with collagen that makes the repairing process faster. It tightens your skin and makes it firmer and smoother. Your wrinkles will disappear within three weeks of the treatment.
Open Pores
  • Open pores are a common problem for the people with oily skin. It is due to the excessive oil and dirt that sit up on the skin and around the pore areas, making the skin thicker. PRP treats the large pours very well as it promotes the regeneration of the skin cells that help you lessen the depth of the pores.

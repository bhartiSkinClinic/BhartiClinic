Skin Pigmentation
Forget Hiding. Get back the skin you love to flaunt with Skin colour is because of melanin pigment which is produced inside skin. Excess of melanin production may cause dark patches on your skin. This excess melanin production occurs due to many factors.
Dark patches on skin may occur anywhere on the body, but exposed body parts such as the face, upper back, hand, around the eyes, lips and underarms bothers the most. Why do we get dark spots on skin?
Dark spot or pigmentation over skin occurs because of excess melanin pigment present in skin. Melanin pigment is continuously produced in the skin. There are several factors interfere in normal production of melanin resulting in excess production and dark spots.
To name a few, they are :
  • UV radiation
  • Drugs
  • Other skin condition
  • Vitamin / nutritional deficiency
  • Harmonal
  • Genetic predisposition
